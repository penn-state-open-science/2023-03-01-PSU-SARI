Sharing image & video data

The challenge

Images/video of people pose risks of identification

(One) solution




Alter images/videos

But, altering images/video…




is time/resource consuming

makes images/video less valuable for reuse by others

might pose risks to participants (if using external service)

(Another) solution

Share with researchers not with the public

Researchers with ethics training

Researchers subject to institutional supervision

Share with participant permission

Share only with research team members OR

Share with other researchers OR

Share with other researchers + allow clips to be shown OR

Share publicly

Restricted access data repository

Housed at NYU; staff at NYU & PSU
